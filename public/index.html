Privacy Policy

Data we collect: Name, email, mobile number, your selected event category/distance, and — once you connect Strava — your activity data (type, distance, elapsed time, date, and GPS presence) for activities matching your registered event window.

How we use it: To verify your event participation (distance and consistency checks) and to calculate and display Global Leaderboard rankings.

Sharing with other athletes: We only display your total distance, average pace, and consistency status on the public Global Leaderboard if you have explicitly consented to this when connecting your Strava account. You may withdraw this consent at any time by contacting us. We never share your raw Strava activity data, GPS routes, or personal contact details with other athletes.

Data retention: Your data is retained for the duration of the event and a reasonable period afterward for results verification. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time via the "Delete My Account" option in your dashboard, or by contacting us.

Strava data specifically: If you disconnect or revoke Strava access (via Strava's own settings or our app), we stop syncing new activities and clear your stored Strava tokens immediately. Previously synced activity records are deleted along with your account upon deletion request.

Security: Passwords are hashed (never stored in plain text). Strava access tokens are stored server-side and never exposed to other users.

Contact: For any privacy questions or data requests, contact challenge21x21@gmail.com.

Terms of Service

Eligibility: By registering, you confirm the information you provide is accurate and that you are participating voluntarily and at your own risk.

Strava connection: Connecting your Strava account is mandatory to participate. You are responsible for keeping your Strava profile public so your activities can be verified, and for the accuracy of the activities you log.

Fair play: Manually-entered Strava activities (no device/GPS data) are not counted toward your consistency streak or leaderboard ranking. Attempting to manipulate rankings through fraudulent activity data may result in disqualification.

Leaderboard display: If you consent to leaderboard data sharing, your total distance, pace, and consistency status will be visible to other registered athletes for the duration of the event.

Account termination: You may delete your account at any time. We may suspend or remove accounts found to violate fair play rules or these terms.

Changes: These terms may be updated from time to time; continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
